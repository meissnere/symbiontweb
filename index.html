For 1/3 of the elderly diagnosed with a neuro-degenerative disease,
living independently in their own homes is very important (US AoA).

We want to support their independence for as long as possible.
Therefore, we've introduced the Pillit: a 3-part solution to increase prescription adherence.

We've developed for our own grandparents, and they love it!

At the Arden Courts of Kensington, a memory care community,
we have received feedback from almost 40 patients. Here are the values we highlight in our solution:

  • Increased Adherence

    Complying with your medications betters your health. The NIH published with 95% certainty that increased adherence was associated with a statistically significant improvement in outcomes.

  • Save Taxpayer Money

    Prescription non-adherence costs the US roughly $330 billion a year in unnecessary medical complications. The opportunity for savings here is enormous!

  • Support Independence

    We believe that people with dementia who want to remain in their own homes should be supported to do so for as long as possible. Pillit was designed to enable seniors to keep their independence.

  • Ease of Mind

    We are grandchildren of independent patients with neuro-degenerative diseases and understand how worrisome the situation is. That's why we keep loved ones connected.

  • Alert System

    If the patient does not take their medication in one hour, their loved ones receive a text alert. Adherence information is made available to the patient's physician for improved health insights!
